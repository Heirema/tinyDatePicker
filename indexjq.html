Tiny date picker

Looking for tiny foot print, fast, scaleable, flexible and pluggable...
This 4.91KB (gZip; 11.79KB minified) small date picker provides a lot of hooks for developers to write plugins, calendars, agendas, etc. (See demo plugins below in Demo).
